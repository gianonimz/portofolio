WHO I AM

Hello, I'm Gian.

I'm a husband, father, content creator, and storyteller from Indonesia. I write about the things people experience every day but rarely talk about honestly—social issues, internet culture, technology, creator economy, and ordinary life. Most of my content starts with one simple thought: "What if I'm not the only one feeling this?"
